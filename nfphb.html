The Not-for-Profit Handbook

I spent seven years helping run a small Not-for-Profit in Melbourne and technical consulting to many others. It is a chapter I have largely left behind but as part of a wrap-up process I started collaborating on a handbook for modern Not-for-Profits. The other author decided he wanted to change direction with the book but I wanted to do something with some of the work I had undertaken. I will merge some other resources into this section over time.
