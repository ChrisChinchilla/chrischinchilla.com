# My Content Creator & Music Production Setup

A rundown of the gear across my desk and studio space.

---

## 📷 Camera Setup

### Panasonic Lumix G Series Camera

The centerpiece of the camera rig is a **Panasonic Lumix G** mirrorless camera (likely the G7 or G85), mounted between two monitors on a desk clamp bracket. It's paired with a **Lumix G Vario kit lens** with the lens cap on. A popular choice for video content creators thanks to 4K recording and a compact Micro Four Thirds body.

🔗 [Panasonic Lumix G85 on Amazon](https://www.amazon.de/s?k=Panasonic+Lumix+G85)

---

### Elgato Key Light

Sitting on the camera's hot shoe mount is an **Elgato Key Light** — a compact, app-controlled LED panel designed for content creators. It delivers bright, soft, colour-temperature-adjustable light and is controllable via the Elgato Control Center app or a Stream Deck.

🔗 [Elgato Key Light on Amazon](https://www.amazon.de/s?k=Elgato+Key+Light)

---

### Monitor-Mounted Camera Bracket

The camera sits on a **monitor/desk-mounted camera arm bracket** — a metal clamp that slots between two monitors to position a camera at eye level. Great for webcam replacement setups.

🔗 [Monitor Mount Camera Bracket on Amazon](https://www.amazon.de/s?k=monitor+mount+camera+bracket+between+screens)

---

## 🎹 MIDI Controller

### Novation Launchkey 49

A **Novation Launchkey 49** — a 49-key MIDI keyboard controller in white. It features faders, knobs, drum pads, and deep DAW integration (especially with Ableton Live). The LCD display and chord/scale modes make it a capable production tool.

🔗 [Novation Launchkey 49 on Amazon](https://www.amazon.de/s?k=Novation+Launchkey+49)

---

## 🖥️ Monitors

### Dell 27" UltraSharp (×2)

Flanking the camera are two **Dell 27" UltraSharp monitors** — a matching dual-monitor setup offering plenty of screen real estate for editing, coding, and multitasking. Dell's UltraSharp line is known for colour accuracy and wide viewing angles, making them well suited to both content work and development.

🔗 [Dell 27" UltraSharp on Amazon](https://www.amazon.de/s?k=Dell+27+UltraSharp)

---



### Elgato Stream Deck (Full Size)

The darker unit with a grid of LCD buttons is an **Elgato Stream Deck** (MK.2 or original). Each button is programmable for launching apps, triggering clips, switching scenes, and more.

🔗 [Elgato Stream Deck MK.2 on Amazon](https://www.amazon.de/s?k=Elgato+Stream+Deck+MK2)

---

### Elgato Stream Deck Mini (White)

Next to it is a **Stream Deck Mini** in white — a compact 6-button version of the same concept. Handy as a secondary controller or for a more minimal layout.

🔗 [Elgato Stream Deck Mini on Amazon](https://www.amazon.de/s?k=Elgato+Stream+Deck+Mini)

---

## 🎙️ Desk / Streaming Setup

### Arturia MiniFuse 4

The compact **Arturia MiniFuse 4** audio interface handles XLR/line inputs and audio routing for the setup. It offers four inputs, solid preamps, and a clean driver-free USB-C connection — a great mid-range choice for podcasting, recording, and streaming.

🔗 [Arturia MiniFuse 4 on Amazon](https://www.amazon.de/s?k=Arturia+MiniFuse+4)

---

### Shure SM7B

The microphone is a **Shure SM7B** (or SM7dB) — an industry-standard dynamic broadcast microphone on a desktop stand. Used on countless podcasts, voiceovers, and even music recordings, it delivers a warm, smooth sound and excellent off-axis rejection.

🔗 [Shure SM7B on Amazon](https://www.amazon.de/s?k=Shure+SM7B)

---

### Keychron Mechanical Keyboard

A **Keychron K-series tenkeyless mechanical keyboard** in dark grey, identifiable by the layout and the signature orange Escape keycap. Wireless-capable and popular among developers and writers.

🔗 [Keychron K2 on Amazon](https://www.amazon.de/s?k=Keychron+K2+mechanical+keyboard)

---

### Logitech MX Master 3 Mouse

The mouse is a **Logitech MX Master 3** — an ergonomic productivity mouse with a fast scroll wheel, thumb scroll wheel, and multi-device pairing. A staple for power users.

🔗 [Logitech MX Master 3 on Amazon](https://www.amazon.de/s?k=Logitech+MX+Master+3)

---

### Lamicall Phone / Tablet Stand

There's a **Lamicall phone or tablet stand** in black on the desk — a compact adjustable holder for keeping a device upright at eye level.

🔗 [Lamicall Phone Stand on Amazon](https://www.amazon.de/s?k=Lamicall+phone+stand)

### Elgato 4K Capture Card

Tucked out of sight is an **Elgato 4K60 Pro** (or 4K60 Pro MK.2) capture card, used to route the Lumix G's HDMI output into the Mac as a clean video feed. Supports up to 4K60 HDR passthrough and is what makes the camera usable as a high-quality webcam or recording source.

🔗 [Elgato 4K60 Pro on Amazon](https://www.amazon.de/s?k=Elgato+4K60+Pro)

---

### Apple Mac Mini M4 Pro

The brains of the operation is an **Apple Mac Mini M4 Pro** — Apple's compact desktop powerhouse. The M4 Pro chip offers exceptional multi-core CPU and GPU performance in a tiny footprint, making it ideal for video editing, music production, and software development without the bulk of a tower.

🔗 [Apple Mac Mini M4 Pro on Amazon](https://www.amazon.de/s?k=Apple+Mac+Mini+M4+Pro)
