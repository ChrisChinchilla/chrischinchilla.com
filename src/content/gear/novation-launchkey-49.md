For ten years I used an [Akai MPK Mini 3](https://www.amazon.de/s?k=Akai+MPK+Mini+3), but then recently switched to a Novation Launchkey 49, which also replaced my [Korg nanoKontrol 2](/gear/korg-nanokontrol-2). I am still getting used to using it optimally as it's a lot more complex, but I especially love having a few more octaves to play with.
