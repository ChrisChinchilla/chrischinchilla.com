My main audio interface is an [Arturia Minifuse 4](https://amzn.to/3WZTjlO), which I use for connecting my [Shure MV7](/gear/shure-mv7) as well as [DT 770 Pro](/gear/dt-770-pro) headphones. And when I work on music, I use it to connect guitars and synths, etc. I mostly bought it for the dual headphone outputs, meaning that my musical collaborator and I can have our own headphones and mixes. I don't currently use the MIDI connections, but I may in the future.
